# The Three Circles of $ZSOLAR Ownership

Public narrative framing. Use this language verbatim (or close to it) on the site, in the investor demo, and in any founder/team comms. Order matters — the Pact is always introduced first.

## Circle 1 — The Family Legacy Pact (200B, never sold)

> "Joseph and Michael Tschida hold 200,000,000,000 $ZSOLAR — 20% of the entire supply — under a public, on-chain Family Legacy Pact. **These tokens are never sold. Not at $1. Not at $20. Not ever.** They are the anchor of the network."

- Joseph: 150B pact-locked
- Michael: 50B pact-locked
- Crossover prices ($1T market cap): $6.67 / $20 — **paper milestones, not exit events**
- Framing: "The founders eat last. Their wealth is the network's wealth."

## Circle 2 — Founder Operating Allocation (≤ 30B, vested + rate-limited)

> "Separately from the Pact, the founders hold a small, fully-disclosed Operating Allocation — capped at 3% of supply — that vests over four years with a one-year cliff. This pool exists so the people who invented $ZSOLAR can pay rent, hire, and live without ever touching the Pact. Every unlock and every sale is published on-chain."

- **Size:** up to 30B (3% of supply) — split however the founders agree internally
- **Vesting:** 4-year linear, 1-year cliff (industry standard)
- **Sell limits:** ≤ 0.25% of supply per founder per month after cliff (auto-enforced via vesting contract)
- **Disclosure:** every unlock and every transfer surfaces in the public Founder Activity feed
- **Why it exists:** "Building a trillion-dollar energy network takes a decade. Founders need food and shelter for that decade. We made that line bright and public so no one has to guess."

## Circle 3 — Team & Contributor Pool (50B, 5%)

> "Five percent of $ZSOLAR is reserved for the people who build it with us. Engineers, scientists, energy operators, and contributors earn $ZSOLAR on the same four-year vest with a one-year cliff that the founders use. **At Zen Solar, your equity is liquid the moment it vests — you don't wait for an IPO that may never come.**"

- **Size:** 50B (5% of supply)
- **Vesting:** 4-year linear, 1-year cliff
- **Liquidity:** open-market, immediate post-vest (no lockup beyond cliff)
- **Recruiting line:** "Most private companies pay you in paper. We pay you in a token you can actually use, hold, or sell — backed by real kilowatt-hours."

## Circle 4 — Advisor Pool (10B, 1%)

> "One percent is set aside for advisors who shape the network — energy industry veterans, cryptographers, and policy minds. Two-year vest, six-month cliff."

- **Size:** 10B (1% of supply)
- **Vesting:** 2-year linear, 6-month cliff (shorter because advisor engagements are shorter)

## Why this structure beats a traditional IPO path (use in investor demo)

> "A traditional private company gives founders and employees paper that only becomes real if they IPO or get acquired. Most never do. **Zen Solar inverts that.** The Pact guarantees the founders never dump. The Operating, Team, and Advisor pools — together less than 10% of supply — give the people building the network real, liquid, transparent ownership from day one. We don't need an IPO. The token *is* the IPO, and it started the day we minted the genesis block."

## Hero hierarchy (always render in this order)

1. **Family Legacy Pact — 200B, never sold** (largest visual weight, always first)
2. Founder Operating — 30B, 4yr/1yr cliff, on-chain disclosed
3. Team & Contributors — 50B, 4yr/1yr cliff
4. Advisors — 10B, 2yr/6mo cliff
5. Everything else (LP, treasury, mint rewards) — unchanged from existing tokenomics

## Forbidden phrasings

- ❌ "Founders can sell their tokens" → ✅ "Founders have a small, separate, vested operating allocation"
- ❌ "Founder dump" / "founder unlock" in marketing → ✅ "scheduled vesting event, published on-chain"
- ❌ Treating the Pact and the Operating Allocation as the same pool → always two visually distinct circles
- ❌ Mentioning founder liquidity before the Pact in any paragraph, slide, or section
