# ZPPA — Zen Power Purchase Agreement

The official name for ZenSolar's producer-gated early-access program on every $ZSOLAR LP tranche release. See `mem://features/producer-gated-lp-rounds` for the underlying mechanics.

## Why "ZPPA"
- "PPA" (Power Purchase Agreement) is universally understood in clean-energy circles — utilities, solar installers, grid operators all live and breathe it.
- Adding the **Z** front-loads the ZenSolar brand and makes it ownable/trademarkable.
- Doubles as a wink: traditional PPAs let utilities buy power from producers. **ZPPA lets producers buy the token before whales do.** Inversion of the normal capital flow.
- Sayable, recruitable, fits on a button, fits in a tweet.

## Naming rules
- Always capitalized: **ZPPA**
- Long form on first mention per page: **Zen Power Purchase Agreement (ZPPA)**
- After first mention: just **ZPPA**
- Never "Zen-PPA", "Z.P.P.A.", or "zppa" lowercase
- Never describe ZPPA as a contract the user signs — it's an **earned access tier**

## Where it lives in the app
1. **Wallet page** — small ZPPA badge under the $ZSOLAR balance:
   - Eligible: "✅ ZPPA Active — Producer Priority"
   - Almost: "Mint X more kWh to unlock ZPPA"
   - Not connected: "Connect a clean-energy device to qualify for ZPPA"
2. **Tokenomics / LP rounds page** — full ZPPA explainer, eligibility math, current round size, producer allocation %, leftover-to-public %.
3. **Mint History** — small "Counts toward ZPPA" chip on qualifying mint rows (later phase, not now).
4. **Marketing / landing** — "Earn your way in. ZPPA puts producers first."

## Forbidden phrasings
- ❌ "Anyone can join ZPPA" → ✅ "ZPPA is earned by producing verified clean energy."
- ❌ "Buy into ZPPA" → ✅ "Mint kWh to qualify for the next ZPPA window."
- ❌ "ZPPA holders" → ✅ "ZPPA-eligible producers"
