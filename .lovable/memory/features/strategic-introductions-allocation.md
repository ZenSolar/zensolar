# Strategic Introductions Allocation

## What it is
A pre-launch token bucket Joseph controls personally to reward people who deliver high-value warm introductions (whales, strategic partners, distribution partners, regulators).

- **Total bucket:** 100,000,000 $ZSOLAR (0.01% of 1T supply)
- **Source:** Carved from team pool (team pool reduced from 25B → 24.9B)
- **Tokenomics model version:** v3 (active). v2 archived in `tokenomics_archive` with reason.
- **Default per-intro reward:** 1,000,000 $ZSOLAR (Lyndon-tier)
- **Default vesting:** 12 months linear, 3-month cliff
- **First earmarked intro:** Whoever sets the meeting with Lyndon (Jo Ferriter has first right of refusal — Joseph and Jo go way back)

## Critical distinction: tokens ≠ equity
- These are **pre-launch protocol tokens** ($ZSOLAR), not company equity in ZenCorp Inc.
- Recipients get economic upside on the token, NOT:
  - Voting rights on ZenCorp Inc decisions
  - Anti-dilution protection
  - Information/inspection rights
  - Any claim on the legal entity
- Always frame externally as "pre-launch token allocation" or "$ZSOLAR allocation" — NEVER "equity" or "shares." Equity language creates securities-law and cap-table confusion.
- Internally we may casually call it "skin in the game" but contracts/side letters say **tokens**.

## How to award
1. Conditional in writing (one-page side letter or signed text thread is fine at this stage).
2. Trigger condition is explicit: e.g. "1M $ZSOLAR upon Lyndon (a) taking the meeting AND (b) committing to invest." Pick the trigger; document it.
3. Vest from grant date (or trigger date — pick one and stick with it).
4. Logged internally in cap table + tokenomics_archive trail. Not published on the public tokenomics page.

## Why off the public page
Investors get nervous when finder's fees / intro rewards are publicized. Keep this in internal cap table and `tokenomics_archive` migration history only.

## Why this bucket exists at all
Joseph will need to do this repeatedly as ZenSolar scales. Having a formal bucket means:
- No ad-hoc decisions chipping at other allocations
- Future intros draw from the same well (consistency)
- Clean accounting trail in `tokenomics_models` + `tokenomics_archive`

**Why:** Established 2026-04-25 when Joseph asked to spin up an intro reward for whoever connects him to Lyndon. Bucket sized at 100M (not 50M) to give comfortable headroom for 50–100 future strategic intros at the 1M default.
